## Overview

Pineview Coffee is a specialty coffee brand based in Austin, Texas, known for its ethically sourced beans and artisanal blends. Bryan Hisey commissioned me to craft a professional logo, design and develop a WordPress website, and establish their presence on social media platforms. This project helped Pineview Coffee create a cohesive brand identity and build its online presence to attract coffee enthusiasts and wholesale buyers alike.

## Key Features

- **Logo Design**: Created a modern, sophisticated logo that reflects the premium quality and artisanal nature of Pineview Coffee.
- **WordPress Website**: Developed a responsive website that highlights Pineview Coffee’s product offerings, coffee sourcing story, and contact information. The website includes an integrated blog for sharing coffee-related content and a contact form for wholesale inquiries.
- **Social Media Setup**: Established social media channels on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn, providing a foundation for engaging with coffee lovers and promoting new products.
- **Brand Cohesion**: Ensured a consistent design language across the logo, website, and social media platforms to build a strong and recognizable brand identity.

## Technologies Used

- **Adobe Illustrator**: For designing the logo and branding assets.
- **WordPress**: For building the company website, featuring responsive design and customizable features.
- **GoDaddy**: For domain registration and website hosting.
- **Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn**: For launching and managing social media profiles.
- **Adobe Photoshop**: For creating visuals and mockups for the website and social media content.

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was developing a brand identity that balanced Pineview Coffee’s premium offerings with its approachable, community-focused values. Crafting a user-friendly website while maintaining a high-end aesthetic required close attention to detail. Additionally, setting up engaging social media profiles with cohesive visuals and messaging ensured a seamless brand experience across all platforms.

## Outcome

The completed project successfully launched Pineview Coffee’s online presence and provided Bryan Hisey with a robust foundation for marketing his brand. The logo became a key visual element for the company’s branding, while the website and social media channels offered platforms for engaging with customers and showcasing the company’s offerings. Pineview Coffee’s unified digital presence effectively communicated its values, quality, and commitment to the coffee community.
