## Overview

The project aimed to redesign and enhance ProJob's website by creating a centralized hub that integrates the company's subsidiary staffing agencies—Sterling Hires, MedMasa, Star-1, and Advanced Staffing Associates. The objective was to streamline navigation, improve user experience, and ensure a cohesive web presence for the entire staffing agency network.

## Key Features

- **Centralized ProJob Hub**: Developed a unified parent website that serves as the primary entry point to all subsidiary brands, improving site navigation and user engagement.
- **Brand Integration**: Seamlessly integrated the branding for Sterling Hires, MedMasa, Star-1, RC Staff Inc., Cornerstone Staffing Solutions, and Advanced Staffing Associates into the ProJob hub, ensuring each brand maintains its unique identity while contributing to a cohesive user experience.
- **Responsive Web Design**: Applied mobile-first design principles to ensure a seamless experience across all devices, from desktops to smartphones.
- **Job Search Functionality**: Implemented a powerful job search feature with customizable filters, allowing job seekers to easily find relevant positions across multiple staffing brands.
- **SEO Optimization**: Incorporated SEO best practices to enhance the visibility of ProJob and its subsidiary brands in search engine results, driving more traffic to the websites.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: For content management, providing an easy-to-use platform for content updates and scalability.
- **GoDaddy**: For domain registration and reliable website hosting services.
- **Divi Theme by Elegant Themes**: A highly customizable theme for WordPress that allowed for quick development and easy design flexibility across multiple pages.
- **Google Analytics**: For tracking website performance, user engagement, and optimizing site functionality based on real-time insights.

## Project Milestones

1. **Project Launch**: The project began with domain and DNS research, gathering digital assets, and finalizing the project plan and timeline.
2. **Development of ProJob Hub**: Focused on building the centralized ProJob hub with integrated call-to-action sections linking to each subsidiary brand.
3. **Development of Individual Brand Websites**: Developed the individual websites for Sterling Hires, MedMasa, Star-1, RC Staff Inc., Cornerstone Staffing Solutions, and Advanced Staffing Associates, ensuring a modern and user-friendly design across all brands.
4. **Testing, Optimization, and Team Feedback**: Conducted thorough testing for functionality, performance, and user experience, and implemented necessary optimizations for improved SEO and site speed.
5. **Launch and Post-Launch Support**: Launched the ProJob hub and individual brand websites, offering post-launch support and providing the client’s team with training and documentation for managing the new sites.

## Outcome

The redesigned ProJob website now provides a cohesive, user-friendly experience that seamlessly integrates the company’s subsidiary brands. The centralized hub has improved site navigation and visibility for all staffing agencies, and the new job search functionality has made it easier for users to find job opportunities. Post-launch support and training have ensured that the client team is equipped to manage and update the website efficiently, helping ProJob stay competitive in the staffing industry.
