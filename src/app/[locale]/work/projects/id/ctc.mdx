## Overview

Certified Tree Care is a professional tree care company based in Austin, Texas, specializing in tree trimming, removal, and health management. Bryan Hisey commissioned me to create a professional logo, develop a WordPress website, and establish a social media presence to enhance the company’s visibility and customer engagement. This project laid the foundation for Certified Tree Care’s long-term success in the industry.

## Key Features

- **Logo Design**: Designed a professional and memorable logo representing the company’s expertise in tree care and its commitment to sustainability.
- **WordPress Website**: Developed a responsive and user-friendly website using the Divi theme on WordPress, hosted on GoDaddy. The site included key service information, contact forms, and a gallery of completed projects to attract potential customers.
- **Social Media Setup**: Established official social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube, providing a platform for engaging with the community and showcasing services.
- **Brand Cohesion**: Ensured consistency in design and messaging across all platforms to build a unified and recognizable brand identity.

## Technologies Used

- **Adobe Illustrator**: For creating the logo design and branding assets.
- **WordPress**: For building the company website, leveraging the Divi theme for customization and ease of use.
- **GoDaddy**: For domain registration and website hosting.
- **Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube**: For launching and managing social media platforms.
- **Adobe Photoshop**: For creating visual content and mockups for the website and social media posts.

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was ensuring seamless integration between the website and the newly launched social media channels, allowing for cross-promotion and consistent branding. Additionally, designing a logo that conveyed both professionalism and approachability required careful attention to detail. This project emphasized the importance of a well-rounded digital presence in driving customer engagement.

## Outcome

The completed project successfully launched Certified Tree Care’s online presence, helping the company establish itself as a trusted name in the tree care industry. The logo became a cornerstone of their branding, while the website and social media channels provided a platform for reaching new clients and building community relationships. Bryan Hisey expressed satisfaction with the results, which laid the groundwork for the company’s continued growth and visibility.
