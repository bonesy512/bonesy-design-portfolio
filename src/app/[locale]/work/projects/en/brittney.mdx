## Overview

Brittney Cassel commissioned a personalized digital sticker that captured her likeness in a fun, cartoon-style design. This project involved taking a digital photograph of Brittney and transforming it into a vibrant illustration using Adobe Illustrator. The resulting design was tailored for use as a sticker and for other personalized memorabilia.

## Key Features

- **Custom Illustration**: Digitally recreated Brittney's portrait with a cartoon-style aesthetic, ensuring the design was both playful and recognizable.
- **Sticker-Ready Design**: Optimized the illustration for printing as high-quality stickers, making it versatile for personal and promotional use.
- **Adobe Illustrator Expertise**: Utilized vector-based tools to create a scalable and detailed design with clean lines and vibrant colors.

## Technologies Used

- **Adobe Illustrator**: For designing and vectorizing the custom cartoon-style portrait.
- **Adobe Photoshop**: For editing and refining the original digital photograph before illustration.
- **Premium Sticker Printing Specs**: Ensured the design was optimized for high-quality printing.
