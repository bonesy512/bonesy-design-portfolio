## Overview

Adams Hive Estates is an upcoming residential subdivision located in Poteet, Texas, featuring 21 one-acre lots and 154 30,000 sq. ft. lots. This project required the development of a compelling brand identity and a comprehensive digital marketing campaign to establish the subdivision’s presence in the market. My role focused on creating the brand standards, producing high-quality marketing materials, and executing social media strategies to assist Anthony Gaona in generating leads and driving property sales.

## Key Features

- **Brand Standards and Guidelines**: Developed a cohesive brand identity, including logos, color palettes, typography, and a comprehensive style guide. These standards ensured consistency across all digital platforms and marketing materials.
- **Digital Marketing Assets**: Designed visually engaging brochures, flyers, email templates, and advertisements tailored for both online and print campaigns. These assets were optimized for lead generation and brand awareness.
- **Social Media Content**: Produced platform-specific content for Facebook and Instagram, including static posts, animated graphics, and video highlights, to attract and engage potential buyers.
- **3D Neighborhood Animation**: Created a 3D fly-around video of the subdivision using Blender, providing a stunning visual overview of the neighborhood to enhance promotional efforts and showcase the property layout.
- **Community Highlights**: Emphasized the serene lifestyle and spacious lot sizes offered by Adams Hive Estates, appealing to prospective homeowners seeking a balance of tranquility and accessibility.

## Technologies Used

- **Blender**: For creating a 3D fly-around animation of the neighborhood.
- **Unreal Engine 5**: For developing immersive, interactive visuals of the subdivision.
- **Adobe Premiere Pro & After Effects**: For producing high-quality promotional videos and animations.
- **Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop**: For crafting logos, branded materials, and marketing content.
- **Vercel**: For hosting landing pages and managing web-based elements of the project.
- **GitHub**: For version control and collaboration on web components.
- **Envato Elements & KitBash3D**: For sourcing premium assets and 3D models to enhance visuals.
- **Slack**: For seamless communication and coordination with the team.
- **Google Workspace**: For managing project documents, timelines, and marketing strategies.

## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was crafting a unique identity for Adams Hive Estates in a competitive real estate market. By highlighting the subdivision’s spacious lot sizes and location in Poteet, Texas, I successfully differentiated it from other developments. Additionally, integrating immersive 3D visualizations into the marketing strategy required advanced coordination between branding and technical design tools, ultimately elevating the project’s appeal.

## Outcome

The branding and marketing efforts established Adams Hive Estates as a standout residential community. The social media campaigns and lead generation strategies attracted significant interest from potential buyers, while the 3D neighborhood animation added an immersive and professional touch to the promotional materials. These efforts provided Anthony Gaona with the tools and visibility needed to effectively market the properties, resulting in a steady flow of leads and growing engagement.
